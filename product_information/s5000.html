Series | 5000 CardModules

Series 5000 is a modular based terminal equipment solution. We provide 2RU and 1RU 19" Rack Chassis which are populated with a variety of modules to achieve the desired functionality. The system includes optional redundant power protection and a central control system.

Below is a listing of the available modules and links to the associated catalog and specification sheets. The compatibility column indicates compatibility with 270Mbit / 1.5Gbit / 3Gbit and Fiber Optic signals.
