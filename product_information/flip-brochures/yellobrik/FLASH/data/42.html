The OTR 1840 is a CWDM Fiber Optic to SDI transmitter and receiver combined 

in a compact self contained package. It is a convenient and cost effective 

solution to combat the restrictions involved with the distribution of uncompressed 

broadcast quality video signals over long distances.

Each OTR 1840 CWDM tranceiver has an independant transmitter and receiver 

channel, which provides an effective solution for any SDI signal up to 1080p60 

(3Gbit/s) while preserving full uncompressed quality. Select from 18 transmitter 

wavelengths for full CWDM compatibility (ITU-T G.694.2)

The OTR 1840 will auto-detect and re-clock any 270Mbit / 1.5Gbit and 3Gbit SDI 

source prior to conversion. The module is fully compatible with 3Gbit Level A and 
Level B formats.
