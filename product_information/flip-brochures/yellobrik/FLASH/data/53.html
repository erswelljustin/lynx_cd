RFR 1018

19” 0.5RU Mounting Tray

• Small footprint only 0.5 RU High x 19” Rack mount
• For use with: OCM 1891 / 1892
                         OCM 1842 / 1843 / 1844
                         OSP 1812 / 1812M / 1814
                         OTX 1441 / OTR 1441
• Easy module mounting - no tools needed
• Combine with RFR 1000 frame for system use

The RFR 1018 Mounting Tray is designed to accommodate a variety of LYNX yellobrik 

modules providing a secure mounting platform in any standard 19” rack.

Modules are easily installed from the front and held securely in place a thumbscrew.

When combined with the RFR 1000 Chassis (which can accommodate up to 14 fiber 

yellobriks) a fully featured 18 channel modular CWDM system can be accommodated in 

a total of 1.5RU rack space - see below.
