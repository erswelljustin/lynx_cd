CHD 1802 Application

An example application is shown below, using the CHD 1802 to convert the HDMI output 

from a video camera into SDI, and transmit the SDI over fiber to a remote location. A CDH 

1813 SDI to HDMI converter is used to provide the SDI output and an HDMI monitoring 

output in the remote location with audio, metadata and timecode overlay.
