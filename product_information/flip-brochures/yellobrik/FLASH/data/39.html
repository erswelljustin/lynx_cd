ORX 1900

Fiber to L-Band Receiver

The ORX 1900 is a compact fiber optic receiver for RF signals within the 

extended L-Band range of 700MHz to 2300MHz.

When paired with the OTX 1910 L-Band transmitter you have a very cost 

effective L-Band optical transmitter/receiver system for long distance 

applications.
