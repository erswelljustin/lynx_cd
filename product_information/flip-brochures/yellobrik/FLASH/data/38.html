OTX 1910

L-Band to Fiber Transmitter

The OTX 1910 is an effective solution for transporting analog RF L-Band 

signals over long distances. The module supports RF signals within the 

extended L-Band range of 700MHz to 2300MHz. 

The module is configured using the integrated dip switch. This allows the 

LNB power to be activated and switched to 13V or 18V to select horizontal or 

vertical polarization. Additionally, the internal 22kHz tone generator can be 

turned on and off to select the high or low frequency range.

When paired with the fiber optic receiver for RF signals (yellobrik ORX 1900) 

you have a very cost effective optical RF transmitter/receiver system for 

distances up to 10km.
