Our award winning yellobriks is a new range of portable modular solutions suitable for a variety of applications. Typically used standalone, multiple modules can also be organized and rack mounted in a 1RU Rack mount with primary and redundant power.

Ease of use is a major feature of yellobriks, adjustments and settings are simple, and everything is written right on the module.
